Open Device Lab Kolding

A place where everybody is free to come and test their work on a wide range of devices.

What

Open Device Lab Kolding is an open lab, where web or app developers can test their sites or applications on a wide range of devices. The lab is free and open to everybody.

Why

The web is no longer just on desktop computers. The amount of web traffic from mobile devices is exploding, but the range and fragmentation of mobile devices is constantly growing.

Take part

If you have an old phone that you no long use, or in any other way is willing to help us make the lab better, feel free to write to jakob@substancelab.com.
